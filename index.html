Welcome to the Brotherhood

"Morning Sun Football Club (MSFC) is a football club for the people who love football, specially for the seasoned players of the locality. We believe in brotherhood, fitness, and the timeless passion for the game that brings us together every morning."

Our Mission

To foster a premier football community in Dhaka where seasoned players unite through an unbreakable passion for the game, promoting sportsmanship and strategic excellence.

Our Vision

To be the leading brotherhood for amateur football in Bangladesh, proving that the spirit of the game inspires players to remain active and connected at any age.
